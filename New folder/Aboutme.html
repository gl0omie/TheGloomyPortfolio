About Me

Hello and welcome to my carnival! My name is Desiree, but my art alias is Gloomy Carnival. I am a 20 year old college student studying at Montgomery College, majoring in Graphic Design/Illustration.
I love art and cartoons, and drawing is one of my favorite things to do. I have been doing digital art for about 6 years now and still have plenty of room to grow.
I want to pursue a career in Illustration because I have too many ideas in my head! I want to plug these ideas into something, so art is my outlet. I love drawing fashion ideas, cute people, and bright colors. I wish to one day be able to show my art to the world.
I post my art only on Instagram and TikTok currently and am going to try for  new areas to show my work.
